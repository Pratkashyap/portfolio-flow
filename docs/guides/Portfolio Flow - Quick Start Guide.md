# Portfolio Flow - Quick Start Guide

**Get your professional portfolio live in 5 minutes!**

---

## 🚀 Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Prerequisites
- ✅ Manus account (free to create)
- ✅ Your resume (PDF or text file)
- ✅ 5 minutes of your time

---

## The 4-Step Process

### Step 1️⃣: Upload Resume (2 min)
1. Click **"Get Started"** on the landing page
2. Sign in with your Manus account
3. **Drag and drop** your resume or click to browse
4. Wait for AI to extract your data (5-10 seconds)
5. Review and edit your name, email, phone if needed
6. Click **"Continue"**

**File Requirements:**
- Format: PDF or Text
- Size: Max 10MB
- Content: Min 50 characters

---

### Step 2️⃣: Answer Questions (1-2 min)
1. Answer 7 simple questions about your style
2. Questions include:
   - Job title
   - Years of experience
   - Work style preference
   - Key achievement
   - Design preference
   - Favorite colors
   - Industry
3. Click **"Continue"**

**Tips:**
- Be honest about preferences
- Be specific about achievements
- These customize your design, not your data

---

### Step 3️⃣: Choose Template (1 min)
1. See 3 professional templates:
   - **Minimalist** - Clean & professional
   - **Bold & Modern** - Contemporary & eye-catching
   - **Creative Showcase** - Artistic & expressive
2. Click **"Select & Continue"** on your choice

**Tip:** You can change templates later!

---

### Step 4️⃣: Publish & Share (1 min)
1. Review your portfolio preview
2. Click **"Generate & Publish"**
3. Wait 10-30 seconds for deployment
4. Get your unique URL: `https://[name]-[id].manus.space`
5. Share on LinkedIn, Twitter, or email
6. Done! 🎉

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Resume Upload Failed
- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Try text format instead of PDF
- Ensure at least 50 characters

### Portfolio Not Loading
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser

### Missing Information
- Check original resume
- Re-upload resume
- Edit manually in dashboard
