### File Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Format** | PDF (.pdf) or Text (.txt) |
| **Size** | Maximum 10MB |
| **Content** | Minimum 50 characters |
| **Required Info** | Name, email, phone, experience, education, skills |

### How to Upload

**Method 1: Drag & Drop**
1. Locate your resume file on your computer
2. Click and hold the file
3. Drag it over the upload area
4. Release when you see the blue highlight
5. Wait 5-10 seconds for processing

**Method 2: Click to Browse**
1. Click anywhere in the upload area
2. Your file browser will open
3. Select your resume file
4. Click "Open"
5. Wait 5-10 seconds for processing

### Tips for Best Results

✅ Use a **clear, well-formatted resume**  
✅ Include your **name, email, and phone number**  
✅ List **work experience** with company names and job titles  
✅ Add your **education** with degree and school names  
✅ Include your **skills** and key achievements  
✅ Keep content **organized and easy to read**  

### What Happens After Upload

Portfolio Flow's AI will automatically:

- ✅ Extract your **name and contact information**
- ✅ Identify your **work experience** and job titles
- ✅ List your **technical skills**
- ✅ Extract your **education details**
- ✅ Create a **professional summary**

### Common Issues & Solutions

**"File is too large"**
- Your file exceeds 10MB
- Try compressing the PDF or removing images
- Or convert to a text file instead

**"Failed to extract text from PDF"**
- The PDF might be image-based (scanned document)
- Try converting to a text file instead
- Or re-save the PDF with text layer

**"Resume content is too short"**
- Your resume needs at least 50 characters
- Add more details to your work experience and education

### After Successful Upload

Once your resume is processed:

1. **Review the extracted information**
2. **Edit your Name, Email, Phone** if needed
3. **Click "Continue"** to proceed to the Questionnaire

## Questionnaire

### What You'll See

A personalization form with:

- **Progress Indicator**: "Step 2 of 4"
- **7 Simple Questions**: About your professional style and preferences
- **Question Types**: Text inputs, radio buttons, dropdown menus
- **Continue Button**: To proceed to template selection

### The 7 Questions

**Question 1: What's your job title?**
- Type: Text input
- Example: "Data Analyst", "Product Manager", "Software Engineer"
- Purpose: Helps personalize portfolio tone

**Question 2: Years of experience?**
- Type: Radio buttons
- Options: 0-2, 2-5, 5-10, 10+ years
- Purpose: Determines seniority level display

**Question 3: Your work style?**
- Type: Radio buttons
- Options: Collaborative, Independent, Data-Driven, Creative, Balanced
- Purpose: Influences portfolio messaging

**Question 4: Key achievement?**
- Type: Text input
- Example: "Led analytics strategy driving 8% engagement uplift"
- Purpose: Highlights your proudest accomplishment

**Question 5: Design preference?**
- Type: Radio buttons
- Options: Modern & Professional, Minimalist & Clean, Bold & Creative
- Purpose: Influences template selection

**Question 6: Favorite color?**
- Type: Dropdown menu
- Options: Navy & Mint, Slate & Blue, Purple & Pink, Other
- Purpose: Customizes color scheme

**Question 7: Your industry?**
- Type: Dropdown menu
- Options: Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Creative, Other
- Purpose: Tailors portfolio styling

### Tips

✅ **Be honest** about your preferences  
✅ **Be specific** when describing achievements  
✅ **Choose colors** that represent your brand  
✅ These answers **don't affect your resume data** – they only customize design  

### After Answering

Click **"Continue"** to proceed to Template Selection.

## Success & Sharing

### What You'll See

A **Success page** displaying:

- ✅ **Confirmation Message**: "Your portfolio is now live!"
- **Your Unique Portfolio URL**: The link to your live portfolio
- **Copy Link Button**: To copy the URL to clipboard
- **Social Sharing Buttons**: LinkedIn, Twitter, Email
- **View My Portfolio Button**: To see your live site

### Your Portfolio URL

Your portfolio is hosted at a unique Manus subdomain:

```
https://[your-name]-[user-id].manus.space
```

**Examples:**
```
https://rubi-saini-1.manus.space
https://john-doe-42.manus.space
https://sarah-smith-99.manus.space
```

### Important Information

✅ Your portfolio is **live and accessible** to anyone with the link  
✅ Your portfolio is **hosted for free** on Manus  
✅ Your portfolio is **always available** (no expiration)  
✅ You can **edit and update** your portfolio anytime  
✅ You can **create multiple portfolios** if needed  

### Sharing Options

Portfolio Flow provides multiple ways to share your portfolio:

#### Option 1: Copy Link
- Click **"Copy Link"** button
- URL is copied to your clipboard
- Paste anywhere you want to share it

#### Option 2: Share on LinkedIn
- Click **"Share on LinkedIn"** button
- Opens LinkedIn with a pre-filled post
- Add a personal message if desired
- Click "Post"

#### Option 3: Share on Twitter
- Click **"Share on Twitter"** button
- Opens Twitter with a pre-filled tweet
- Customize the message
- Click "Tweet"

#### Option 4: Share via Email
- Click **"Share via Email"** button
- Opens your email client
- Pre-filled with portfolio link
- Add recipient and personal message
- Send

### Sharing Best Practices

**For LinkedIn:**

**For Twitter:**

**For Email:**

**For Recruiters:**
- Include portfolio link in your resume

### Resume Upload Issues

**"File is too large"**
- Reduce file size to under 10MB
- Remove images or compress PDF
- Convert to text file

**"Failed to extract text"**
- PDF might be image-based
- Try text file format instead
- Re-save PDF with text layer

**"Content is too short"**
- Add more details to resume
- Minimum 50 characters required
- Include work experience and education

### Portfolio Display Issues

**"Portfolio not loading"**
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser

**"My information is missing"**
- Check original resume for data
- Re-upload resume
- Manually edit in dashboard

**"URL is not working"**
- Copy URL again carefully
- Try from different device
- Wait 30 seconds for deployment

### Account Issues

**"I forgot my Manus password"**
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Follow email instructions to reset
- Create new password

**"I can't sign in"**
- Check your email is correct
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cookies
