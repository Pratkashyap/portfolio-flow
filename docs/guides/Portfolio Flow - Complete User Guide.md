## Getting Started

### What is Portfolio Flow?

Portfolio Flow is a **free, easy-to-use portfolio generator** that transforms your resume into a beautiful, professional website. In just a few minutes, you can:

- ✅ Upload your resume (PDF or text file)
- ✅ Answer a quick personality questionnaire
- ✅ Choose from 3 professional templates
- ✅ Get a live portfolio website hosted on Manus
- ✅ Share your portfolio with anyone

### Requirements

- A Manus account (free to create)
- Your resume in PDF or text format
- 5-10 minutes of your time

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI-Powered Resume Parsing** | Automatically extracts your name, email, phone, experience, skills, and education |
| **Personalization Questionnaire** | 7 simple questions to customize your portfolio's style and tone |
| **3 Professional Templates** | Minimalist, Bold & Modern, and Creative Showcase designs |
| **Free Hosting** | Your portfolio is hosted on Manus with a unique subdomain |
| **Social Sharing** | Share your portfolio on LinkedIn, Twitter, and via email |
| **GitHub Integration** | Optional: Display your GitHub repositories and contribution stats |

## Step 2: Resume Upload

### What You'll See

The Resume Upload page displays:

- **Upload Area**: A drag-and-drop zone for your resume
- **File Requirements**: Information about supported formats and file size limits
- **Tips Section**: Best practices for resume content
- **Progress Indicator**: Shows you're on Step 1 of 4

### File Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Supported Formats** | PDF (.pdf) or Text (.txt) files |
| **Maximum File Size** | 10MB |
| **Minimum Content** | At least 50 characters of text |
| **Required Information** | Name, email, phone, work experience, education, skills |

### How to Upload Your Resume

**Option 1: Drag and Drop**
1. Click and hold your resume file
2. Drag it over the upload area
3. Drop it when you see the blue highlight
4. Wait for processing (usually 5-10 seconds)

**Option 2: Click to Browse**
1. Click anywhere in the upload area
2. Select your resume file from your computer
3. Click "Open"
4. Wait for processing

### What Happens Next

Once uploaded, Portfolio Flow's AI will:
- ✅ Extract your name and contact information
- ✅ Identify your work experience and job titles
- ✅ List your technical skills
- ✅ Extract your education details
- ✅ Create a professional summary

### Tips for Best Results

- Use a **clear, well-formatted resume**
- Include your **name, email, and phone number**
- List your **work experience** with company names and job titles
- Add your **education** with degree and school names
- Include your **skills** and key achievements
- Keep content **organized and easy to read**

### Common Issues

**"File is too large"**
- Your file exceeds 10MB
- Try compressing the PDF or removing images
- Or convert to a text file instead

**"Failed to extract text from PDF"**
- The PDF might be image-based (scanned document)
- Try converting to a text file instead
- Or re-save the PDF with text layer

**"Resume content is too short"**
- Your resume needs at least 50 characters
- Add more details to your work experience and education

### After Upload

Once your resume is processed successfully:
1. Review the extracted information
2. Edit your **Name**, **Email**, and **Phone** if needed
3. Click **"Continue"** to proceed to the Questionnaire

These questions help Portfolio Flow:

- Match your template to your professional style

### Tips

- **Choose colors** that represent your brand

Your portfolio is hosted at a unique Manus subdomain:

```
https://[your-name]-[user-id].manus.space
```

**Example:**
```
https://rubi-saini-1.manus.space
https://john-doe-42.manus.space
```

### Important Information

- ✅ Your portfolio is **always available** (no expiration)

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

### General Questions

**Q: Is Portfolio Flow really free?**
A: Yes! Portfolio Flow is completely free. Your portfolio is hosted on Manus at no cost.

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
A: No! Portfolio Flow is designed for everyone. No coding knowledge required.

**Q: How long does it take to create a portfolio?**
A: Most users complete the process in 5-10 minutes.

**Q: Can I edit my portfolio after creation?**
A: Yes, you can edit your portfolio anytime through your dashboard.

**Q: Can I create multiple portfolios?**
A: Yes, you can create as many portfolios as you want.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, your data is secure and private. Only you can edit your portfolio.

### Resume Upload Issues

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: PDF (.pdf) and Text (.txt) files are supported.

**Q: What's the maximum file size?**
A: Maximum file size is 10MB.

**Q: Why did my PDF fail to extract?**
A: If your PDF is image-based (scanned), try converting it to text format instead.

**Q: What if my resume has special formatting?**
A: Portfolio Flow works best with standard formatting. Remove images and complex layouts for best results.

**Q: Can I upload a resume in other languages?**
A: Yes, Portfolio Flow supports multiple languages.

### Portfolio Display Issues

**Q: Why doesn't my portfolio show all my information?**
A: Make sure your resume includes all relevant information. Portfolio Flow extracts data from your resume.

**Q: Can I customize the portfolio design?**
A: You can choose from 3 templates and customize colors. Additional customization coming soon.

**Q: Why is my portfolio URL so long?**
A: Your URL includes your name and user ID to ensure uniqueness. This is normal.

**Q: Can I get a custom domain?**
A: Custom domains are coming soon. For now, you have a Manus subdomain.

### Sharing & Access

**Q: Can anyone access my portfolio?**
A: Yes, anyone with the link can view your portfolio. It's public by default.

**Q: Can I make my portfolio private?**
A: This feature is coming soon. For now, portfolios are public.

**Q: Will my portfolio appear in search engines?**
A: Yes, your portfolio can be indexed by search engines.

**Q: Can I remove my portfolio?**
A: Yes, you can delete your portfolio from your dashboard anytime.

### Technical Issues

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What should I do?**
A: 
1. Try refreshing the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: My portfolio is loading slowly.**
A: This is usually temporary. Try:
1. Refreshing the page
2. Waiting a few minutes
3. Checking your internet connection

**Q: Some of my resume data is missing.**
A: Portfolio Flow extracts data from your resume. If information is missing:
1. Check your original resume for the data
2. Re-upload your resume
3. Manually edit the information in your portfolio

**Q: My portfolio URL is not working.**
A: 
1. Make sure you copied the URL correctly
2. Check that the URL is in your browser's address bar
3. Try accessing from a different device
4. Contact support if the issue persists
