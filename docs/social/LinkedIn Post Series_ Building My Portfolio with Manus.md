## POST 2: The Process (How I Built It)

So how did I actually build that portfolio?

I started with a simple question: what if I could describe what I want, and the system just... understood it?

Step 1: Uploaded my resume (literally just a PDF)
Step 2: Answered a few questions about my work
Step 3: Watched it generate a live portfolio

No code. No deployment. No waiting.

The magic part? The system extracted everything from my resume—my experience, skills, education—and organized it into something that actually looks professional.

I didn't have to manually format anything. Didn't have to worry about hosting. Didn't have to manage servers or databases.

I just described what I wanted, and it existed.

That's the entire process.
